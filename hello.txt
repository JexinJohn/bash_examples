TRICHUR ((About this soundpronunciation (help·info)), AS WELL AS known by its former name Trichur) is a city and headquarters of the TRICHUR district in Kerala, India. It is the third largest urban agglomeration in Kerala after Kochi and Kozhikode urban areas and the 20th largest in India.
